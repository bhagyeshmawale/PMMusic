Music For Little Mozarts Classroom Course (MLMC)

Fundamentals of Music theory Age Group 3 to 12 years.

Music Concepts like high and low sound, tempo of music,rhythm patterns, type of notes,building of music patterns,softege, the basic of reading music,dance style and history of music.

Junior Piano Course For Teenage Students

Elementry Western Classical Theory, Introduction to music theory. Age Group 13 to 18 Years

Focus on all round Musical Development,including repertoiree work,playing technique, scales, sight reading, aural training and music theory. Individual class plans charted out for each session.

Electronic Music Production

In-depth study of Audio Recording using microphone, editing & DAW (Digital Audio Workshops) such as Logic Pro X.

The Electronic Music Production is a rounded music course which covers many aspect of modern music production. The course is designed to help the student to start with an idea into full-fledged composition and production.
